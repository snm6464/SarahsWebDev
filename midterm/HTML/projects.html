OtherWorld Magazine

In an exploration of text and image, I created a mini magazine titled "Other World", where I explored collage, both digital and physical. It was assembled using Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, and Illustrator and printed to consider how text and image are arranged.

Dress Yourself

A "dressable" hanger lamp with three handmade t-shirt lampshades stored in a closet. The lamp base is stained, laser-cut birch, the closet is stained, wood-glued birch, and the clothing pieces are machine and hand sewn with bead and ribbon adornments. The piece aims to explore the ability to "dress" one's space based on their mood - the three shirts are blue, red, and yellow, some of the more common colors to match mood.
